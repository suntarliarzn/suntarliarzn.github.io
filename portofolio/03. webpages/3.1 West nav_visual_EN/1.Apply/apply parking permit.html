Apply parking permit

Explore our user-friendly Parking Permit program, catering to residents, businesses, and visitors with varied permit options, straightforward online application, and prompt permit delivery.

Overview

Danning's Parking Permit program is designed to provide residents with a convenient way to access designated parking areas within the city. Whether you need a residential parking permit or a business parking permit, our online system makes the application process quick and hassle-free.

Who Can Claim a Parking Permit

Parking permits in Danning are available to the following individuals and entities:

  • Residents of Danning who need parking near their residences.
  • Business owners who require parking for their employees or customers.
  • Visitors who plan to stay in Danning for an extended period.

Please note that fees are subject to change due to specific circumstances, such as location and vehicle types. Contact the Transportation Services Division for further information.

How Much Do You Pay for a Parking Permit

Parking permit fees vary depending on the type and duration of the permit:

  • Residential Permit (Annual): $50
  • Business Permit (Quarterly): $75
  • Visitor Permit (Weekly): $20

How to Apply Online

Online application for a parking permit in Danning is a straightforward process. Here are the 5 steps to apply:

  1. Create an Account: Visit our secure Parking Permit Application portal and create a user account. You will need a valid email address to register.
    2. Register or sign in to My Account
  2. Select Permit Type: Log in to your account and choose the type of parking permit you need based on your eligibility.
  3. Complete the Application: Complete the application form with accurate information and upload any required documentation. You may be required to provide proof of residency, business ownership, or visitor status, depending on the permit type.
  4. Choose Delivery and Pay: your preferred delivery option and proceed to pay the fees.
  5. Submit Your Application: Submit your application electronically through the portal.

How is the Parking Permit Delivered

Parking permits will be delivered electronically via email within 3 business days once your application has been approved and payment has been received. You will receive a digital copy of your permit that you can print and display in your vehicle. In certain restricted zones with specific access controls, physical permits may be required for authorized entry. If you require a physical permit, please request one during the application process by selecting the appropriate option. Physical permits will be mailed to you within 5-7 business days.

If you have any questions or need assistance with the parking permit application process, please do not hesitate to contact our Transportation Services Division through the provided contact information on our website.
