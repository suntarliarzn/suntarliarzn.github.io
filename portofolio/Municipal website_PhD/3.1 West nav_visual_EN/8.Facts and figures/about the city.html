Overview

Our facts and figures infographic illustrates the unique local characteristics, as well as opportunities and challenges facing the City of Danning. It details a wide range of relevant statistics and key subjects about Danning grouped in 4 thematic areas from demographics and population to economy and employment, environment, and social circumstances. The stats are collected from national and local data sources and are based on the most recent data available. All figures and facts are up to date until 31 December 2022, unless stated otherwise.
